How it all Started

Bored with the city life, I decided to emback on a journey up north ( Northern part of Ghana; specifically Tamale,Nyankpala,Walewale,Bolgatanga,Zebilla,Bawku,Pusiga) to explore the cattle business . On my way I stoped at various villages to interact with the people to get a different perspective about life; this made me appeciate life the more .

One thing i noticed was the struggle the women in these villages go through in other to get buyers for their shea butter. Out of pity I decided to buy all the Shea Butter they had in stock to share with friends and family back home.

I later realised I can help these women make enough money to support their families and the community and in the process making some financial gains for myself. So I decide to finance their production process ; when the butter is ready I will package them ready for export.

Serendipity!! the stops at these Villages presented me with a challenge which aided my decision to quit my day job and concentrate on this new adventure( Shea butter bussiness) full time.

Together with the women at the village we came up with a name that illustrate the beauty of our work, this lead to the name "UZURI" meaning "Beauty".

Apart from the women being hardworking in their job, the care and dedication applied on the final output makes our products rich in vitamins which is good for the body and helps your skin to glow. No chemicals added. These beautiful women are behind the shea production and they are not just giving you shea butter, they are giving you "Natural Beauty".

They Chose "UZURI" because Natural is Beautiful!.
